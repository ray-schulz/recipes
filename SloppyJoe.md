title: Sloppy Joe
---

+ 1 lb ground beef, browned
+ 1 cup ketchup
+ 1/4 cup water
+ 1 tbsp brown sugar
+ 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
+ 2 tsp mustard
+ 1/2 tsp garlic powder
+ 1/2 tsp onion powder
+ 1/2 tsp salt

1. Stir all ingredients together. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
